Dosa Recipe

Description

The dosa is a unparalleled masterpiece in terms of simplicity, yum factor, and looks. It is easy to make (relatively) and is also pretty cheap. It can be made anywhere in whatever setting, so lets get cooking!

The Recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Lentils
  • Salt
  • Potatoes
  • Masala !

Steps

  1. Create rice pudding by blending rice and lentils
  2. Create dosa by puytting it on a pan and frying for a bit
  3. Mash poetatoes with masal, pu in dosa that you fried, roll up!
